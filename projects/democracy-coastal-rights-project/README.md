## Democracy: From Theory to Practice – Coastal Rights Project

**Assignment Title:** Team Leader – Coastal Rights Civic Education Project  
**Organization:** Microgovernance Research Initiative (MGR), University of Dhaka  
**Partners:** IFES | USAID  
**Type of Engagement:** Leadership / Civic Education Project (Extracurricular)  
**Duration:** September 2024 – February 2025  
**Location:** Rotnapur, Banskhali, Chattogram, Bangladesh  

---

## Project Overview

Under the **Democracy: From Theory to Practice** program, I led the **Coastal Rights** social action project focusing on voting rights and human rights awareness among climate-vulnerable coastal communities.

The project addressed political disenfranchisement caused by climate-induced displacement, limited access to democratic participation, and weak awareness of civic and electoral rights through community-based civic education and advocacy.
