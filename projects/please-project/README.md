## Plastic Free Rivers and Seas for South Asia (PLEASE Project)

**Assignment Title:** Volunteer – Research, Data Collection & Community Awareness  
**Organization:** Department of Marine Science, University of Chittagong  
**Funding & Partners:** World Bank | UNOPS | SACEP  
**Type of Engagement:** Volunteer  
**Duration:** October 2024 – May 2025  
**Location:** Chattogram City Corporation (41 wards), Bangladesh  

---

## Project Overview

The **Plastic Free Rivers and Seas for South Asia (PLEASE)** project is a regional initiative aimed at reducing plastic pollution in rivers and marine environments. As a volunteer, I supported research, data collection, and community awareness activities under the Department of Marine Science, University of Chittagong.

The project focused on evidence-based research and community engagement to promote responsible plastic waste management and circular economy practices.
