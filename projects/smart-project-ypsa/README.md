## Project Overview

This assignment was part of the **baseline survey** of the **SMART Project** implemented by **Young Power in Social Action (YPSA)**. The baseline aimed to assess **socio-economic conditions, aquaculture practices, climate resilience, and business environments of microenterprises**, providing evidence to guide project planning, implementation, and impact evaluation.

The survey supported data-driven decision-making for interventions targeting sustainable livelihoods, climate-resilient aquaculture, and microenterprise development.
