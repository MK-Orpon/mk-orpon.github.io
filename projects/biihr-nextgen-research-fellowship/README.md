## Project / Fellowship Overview

I was selected as one of the **top 10 fellows from over 300 applicants** under the **BIIHR NextGen Research Fellowship 2024**. As a Research Fellow at the Bangladesh Institute of Innovative Health Research (BIIHR), I conducted **community-based public health research** focused on **youth mental health in Bangladesh**, aiming to generate **data-driven and evidence-based insights** to inform policy, advocacy, and programmatic interventions.

The fellowship combined intensive methodological training with applied research, enabling fellows to design, implement, analyze, and disseminate public health research under expert supervision.
